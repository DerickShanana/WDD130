Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this rafting website is to provide comprehensive information about our white water rafting services, including trip details, booking options, safety guidelines, and customer testimonials. It aims to attract adventure enthusiasts and families looking for an exciting outdoor activity, promote our brand, and increase our online presence to reach a wider audience.

Audience

Our target audience includes:

  • Adventure enthusiasts looking for thrilling outdoor activities.
  • Families seeking fun and safe recreational activities during vacations.
  • Corporate groups interested in team-building experiences.
Scenario Questions:
  1. What kind of adventure activities do you enjoy? Answer: I love activities that involve water and a bit of adrenaline, like rafting and kayaking.
  2. Are you looking for family-friendly options? Answer: Yes, we want something exciting but safe for kids as well.
  3. Does your group require team-building activities? Answer: Yes, we are a corporate group looking to improve teamwork through outdoor challenges.
